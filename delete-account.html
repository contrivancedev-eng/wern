We're sorry to see you leave WERN. This page lets you request deletion of your WERN account and associated data. WERN is developed and operated by Project Liberté. Please review the information below before proceeding.

What happens when you delete your WERN account

  • Your WERN account will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.
  • You can cancel the deletion within 30 days by logging back into the WERN app.
  • After 30 days, your data cannot be recovered from WERN's servers.
